My First Day in France

07/14/22 12:00 PM

We just got to the airbnb we're staying at, and I am exhausted. After a 7 hour plane ride, where I got sick, we had to get driven to the place. The road was pretty bumpy, but we saw some interesting buildings. The place itself is beautiful, with a big pool, and some adorable cats. My favorite is an orange and white tabby named Cabbage Cabbage.

Amboise, France

Seeing the Cathedral of Chartres

07/18/22 11:00 AM

My aunt drove my family and I to go see the Cathedral of Chartres. It was a long drive, and we stopped at a small restaurant for lunch. Afterwards we went to the Cathedral, and it was massive, with beautiful stained glass windows, and a ton of people.

Chartres, France

Visiting the Eiffel Tower

07/20/22 3:00 PM

While we were staying in Paris, we went to go see the Eiffel Tower. The first thing to mention is that it is ginormous. There was a ton of people there, and we had to wait on line for quite a bit. We then took the elevator, which goes up the side and not straight up. Near the top of the tower we toured the little cafe area and took pictures.

Paris, France
